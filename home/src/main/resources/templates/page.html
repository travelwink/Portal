Careers

View our full list of available career opportunities here.

There has never been a more exciting time to start a career in the shipping industry. We face unprecedented change as we strive to meet the environmental regulations impacting the maritime industry. As engine designers our responsibility in this is significant. A career with WinGD has a direct impact on shaping a better world for tomorrow. 

It is a rewarding and challenging experience. Inspired to innovate, collaborate and think beyond boundaries, we are surrounded by brilliant minds and passionate people. 

Our success in this fast-moving environment is based on the drive of our dynamic, innovative employees. With worldwide operations, we work within a diverse and multicultural environment. As a small & flexible company, there is room to grow and opportunity to make a mark within the marine industry and our environment.
